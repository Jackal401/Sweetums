Founder's Biography

Meet the founder of Sweetums, Nick Newport, Sr. When he was in charge of Sweetums, instead of using corn syrup to make candy, he used it to fatten cows so that they would provide more meat for consumers.

Owner's Biography

Meet the current owner of Sweetums, and relative to the founder, Nick Newport, Jr. Under his direction, Sweetums has increased relations between them and consumers through the selling of candy and soda products made from corn syrup.
